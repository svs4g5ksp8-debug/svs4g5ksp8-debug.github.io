Get Stoked!

Here's what we've got cooking for this 2025 - 2026 season!

Snowbird, Utah

Snowbird is a mountain-lover’s mountain: just 29 miles from Salt Lake City Airport yet offering huge vertical drops, 2,500+ acres of terrain, and over 500 inches of legendary powder each year. Whether you’re chasing steep bowls, off-piste thrills or simply a luxurious ski-in/ski-out stay, Snowbird blends serious terrain with upscale resort comforts.

Brighton, Utah

Set at the top of Big Cottonwood Canyon just minutes from Salt Lake City, Brighton delivers a friendly, approachable alpine experience with some of Utah’s best snow and terrain parks for all levels. With an emphasis on fun and accessibility, it’s a place where families, freestyle riders, and casual skiers alike can feel at home—by day or night.

Revelstoke, British Columbia

For the adventurous skier seeking world-class terrain, Revelstoke delivers big: with over 3,100 acres, a vertical drop of 1,713 m, and on average 10.5 metres of snowfall annually. It fuses deep powder, steep lines, and a genuine mountain-town feel—perfect for riders who crave challenge and authenticity.

Banff (Sunshine), Alberta

High up on the Continental Divide in the heart of Banff National Park, Sunshine Village offers over 3,300 acres of skiable terrain spanning beginner slopes to expert big-mountain runs. With glacier views, a tree-line lodge, and terrain awaiting both families and freeride enthusiasts, it’s a destination that escapes the ordinary.

Lake Louise, Alberta

Known as “Canada’s Friendly Giant,” Lake Louise spans 4,200 acres across four mountain faces, offering terrain for every ability—framed by sweeping mountain vistas and glaciers. From mellow tree runs to steeps and bowls, Lake Louise combines scale, variety, and breathtaking scenery in one unforgettable package.

Mt. Tremblant, Quebec

A four-season resort nestled in the Laurentians, Mont-Tremblant blends ski terrain for all levels—over 100 trails across four sides of the mountain—with a charming pedestrian village and year-round activities. Whether you’re skiing, hiking, golfing, or relaxing in the alpine setting, Tremblant offers a complete destination experience with style and energy.
